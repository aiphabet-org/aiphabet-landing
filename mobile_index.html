Your AI Journey Starts Here

Aiphabet aims to bring high-quality artificial intelligence (AI) education to middle and high school students. Through our free online learning platform, we offer comprehensive, interactive, and inclusive AI educational resources backed by research findings on informal education. 

Comprehensive AI curriculum tailored for youths

• No background is required. AI readiness prerequisites (mathematics, probability, statistics, programming) are included in the curriculum. 
• Curriculum development backed by research on informal education. 
• Curriculum curated by AI university professors. 

Animated and interactive content

• Fun, animated videos introducing AI concepts.
• Interactive playgrounds where you can visualize the concepts and build a deep understanding of AI.
• Hands-on AI coding projects on games and real-life applications.

Inclusive community-based learning

• Free and fully online.
• Accessible with multiple languages and modalities of learning.
• Discussion forum where you can interact with your peers.
